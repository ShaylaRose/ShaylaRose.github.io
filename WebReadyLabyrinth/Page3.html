Missing and Confused Story Continued

Sarah faces Jareth again. He looks as intimidating as ever. He pleas in a manipulating voice "Dont you want to stay Sarah? I could give you your friends again, I promise, or why else would you come back? to ask me to bring you hear? Sarah replies strong but boldly "I do not want anything more than to see my friends again. I did not come to stay." Jareth laughs mockingly "Say the words then and I will set you free but you wont be allowed to see your friends. Which is it Sarah?" Jareth holds up the crystal ball enticingly. Sarah thinks to herself for a moment and wonders if her friends even needed her in return. At the same thought she looks in his eyes and says to Jareth "Through dangers untold and hardships unnumbered, I have fought my way here to the castlee beyond the goblin city to take back the child that you have stolen, for my will is as strong as yours and my kingdom is as great. You have no power over me!" Sarah wakes in her room
