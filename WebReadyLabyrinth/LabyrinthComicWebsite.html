Labyrinth Story Part 2

As Sarah grew older she lost the magic in her life. There lurking in the shadows was the goblin king. He was waiting for this moment. Jareth notices Sarah was still arguing with her step mother and used this as a for her to say the spell he so longed for her to say. After a long tiring day her arguement with her step mom outside infront of the house was the breaking point. As she ran up into her room she pulls out her book and says in a low tone voice "I really wish the goblins would come take me away.. right now"
