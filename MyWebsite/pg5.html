Pros On Guinea Pigs

I Can Talk For Hours About The Benefits!

Through a series of pictures I can show you why I fell inlove!

There are too many reasons to count on why I think anyone should get these sweet creatures. They cons would NEVER outweigh the pros BUT if you are still not convinced by these photos below, I want to say a couple of things that my guinea pigs have done for me that I will spend my time repaying. Whenever I am stressed out my guinea pigs see me stressed they sort or purr like a cat when I hold them close. They lick me on my nose and hand and they have NEVER bit me once. I feel there should be an animal appreciation day because like a dog, or a cat, they can make ESA animals. My guinea pigs have done so many things to make me laugh and try to bond with me such as talking to me in their chirps and dancing whenever I put on a really good song. They are worth having as a pet!

I hope I convinced you enough to like guinea pigs and also to adopt one and encourage others to step outside of the norm in buying traditional pets. A guinea pig can fit in anyone's home! Thank you!
