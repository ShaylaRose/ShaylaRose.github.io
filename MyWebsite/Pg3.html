My Guinea Pigs

My Guinea Pigs are my life. They saved me from a lot of PTSD and trauma. They are even inspiring me now to do a webpage on them because they unknowingly did a lot for my mental health. This page is dedicated to them and I want to show you how cute they are of coarse.

The Brown Guinea Pig's Name Is Hazel

Hazel is a little older than piggy her favorite things to do are:

  • Bite the cage when hungry
  • Snuggle for hours
  • Lay on the couch and watch movies
  • Listen to me play piano
  • Listen to calm music
  • Eat everything on the ground
  • Squek when hungry

The Other Is Named Piggy She is the black and white one

Her favorite things do to:

  • Popcorn when I sing to her
  • Run around my room
  • Eat my homework
  • Close her eyes when getting pet
  • Eat Strawberries

They were adopted 3 years ago!
